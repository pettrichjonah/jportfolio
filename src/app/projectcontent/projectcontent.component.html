Stift Zwettl Floristenmeisterklasse 2023/24

In September 2024, I had the honor to photographically accompany the florists masterclass of Stift Zwettl in the final days of their exams. For three full days, I documented the fascinating pieces of work in between the stunning architecture of the Stift Zwettl monastery and church while they prepared the area for the public exhibition.

Kuenringer Apotheke Zwettl

The goal of this cooperation was not only to decorate the inside of the recently opened, new built pharmacy but also to put their well known products into the spotlight. Product photography, portrait shootings and architecture camerawork - this project had it all.
